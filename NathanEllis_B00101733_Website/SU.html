Here at ITB, it has it's own Student Union. The SU runs different events for students like Freshers week, a Halloween and Christmas party, Mystery Tour and sometimes trips abroad like skiing trips. The student Union is located in C-Block for students who need to buy event tickets. The student union also has a room for students to play pool, relax, work and watch TV.
