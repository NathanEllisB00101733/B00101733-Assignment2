ITB has many facilities on campus for students, there are multiple rooms for learning such as lecture rooms, mac labs, workshops for engineering courses and gyms for fitness courses. There is also an area for the ecology and Bothany courses. The campus also has a gym, basketball court, astroturf and pitch for excerise. There is the libary for reserch, quiet study and printing or queries at the SID desk. At C-Block there is a canteen, Student Union facility, on campus nurse and AIB bank.
