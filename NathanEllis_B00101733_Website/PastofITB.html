  • History of ITB

ITB was first built in 1999, the Institute was originally meant to be in Navan but IBM influenced the decision to be built in Blanchardstown, to drum up more employent.

The instittue was meant to be near the shopping centre, though its current location was chosen instead.When it first opened its doors, there were only three courses available! Which were: Electronic and Computer Engineering, Buissness Studies and Computing in IT.

Rumour has it that the cement wasnt even fully dry when the college first opened. At the time, the only Building at the time was A-block.

In 2004 the institute was officially Launched.

In 2006 the ITB logo got updated to the current version, as the older verison was hard to work with.

In 2009, ITB celebrated its 10th anniversary by having a comunity day with 10 year old school students as ITB was 10 aswell. Also a staff reunion was held.
